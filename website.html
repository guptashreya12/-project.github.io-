About Us

Do you ever feel like your money is slipping through your fingers faster than you can count it? Do you find yourself living paycheck to paycheck, wondering where all your hard-earned cash went? If so, you might be suffering from a lack of financial literacy. But don't worry, financial literacy doesn't have to be boring or intimidating. In fact, it can be pretty exciting!

We at Finivesta endeavour to bolster students' financial knowledge through multiple events, workshops, riveting competitions, and knowledge-sharing interactive sessions. Numerous events have been organized catering to a wide range of finance-related interests, including investment banking, private wealth management, sales and trading, and corporate finance.

Our society is dedicated to helping students gain a better understanding of finance and investment concepts, and providing them with opportunities to network with professionals in the field. We aim to create a community where students can learn from each other and grow their skills and knowledge.

Finivesta, established in 2019 is open to all students who are interested in finance, regardless of their major or experience level. Whether you are a freshman with a budding interest in finance or a senior who is preparing to enter the workforce, we have something to offer you. Through our events, workshops, and guest speaker series, we strive to bring relevant and up-to-date information to our members.

We organize a variety of activities, including stock market simulations, case competitions, and networking events with finance professionals. Our society also provides opportunities for members to connect with alumni and other professionals who can offer guidance and career advice.
